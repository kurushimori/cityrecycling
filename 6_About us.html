In cities, the amount of domestic and industrial waste generated every day is huge. The sorting, recycling and reuse of garbage have played an indispensable role in urban construction and maintenance. This site is a non-profit website for trial operation. Taking Charlottesville as an example, it aims to provide an online recycling site search platform for the situation of cities and surrounding communities.

At the same time, there are many non-fixed seasonal recycling sites in cities. They usually hold temporary recycling events at some time. This website collects relevant information about special recycling activities, and marks the location on the map to facilitate citizens to find and plan their own recycling of used items.
